Welcome to Radha ki Rasoi (Radha's Kitchen), where authentic Indian gourmet dining meets celebrity hospitality. Founded by Radha (Sophia) Bangaru, a celebrated actress from South India, our intimate dining experiences bring the rich flavors and traditions of Indian cuisine to life in a warm, personal setting.

The Story Behind Radha ki Rasoi

Born and raised in the vibrant culinary landscape of South India, Radha discovered her passion for cooking at an early age, learning traditional recipes from her grandmother and mother. After achieving success as a leading actress in Telugu cinema, including starring roles in popular TV serials and films like "Anaganaga O Prema Katha," Radha decided to share her love for authentic Indian cuisine with the world.

"Food is not just nourishment—it's a celebration of culture, tradition, and love. Every dish tells a story, and I want to share these stories with you." - Radha (Sophia) Bangaru

Our Philosophy

At Radha ki Rasoi, we believe in the power of authentic, well-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Every dish is prepared with love, using family recipes passed down through generations and the finest ingredients sourced from local markets and specialty suppliers.

Our dining experiences are designed to be intimate and personal, allowing guests to not only taste exceptional food but also learn about the cultural significance and stories behind each dish. Whether you're a connoisseur of Indian cuisine or trying it for the first time, we promise an unforgettable culinary journey.

What Makes Us Special

  • Celebrity Chef Experience: Dine with a renowned actress who brings her passion for authentic Indian cuisine to every meal
  • Traditional Recipes: Family recipes passed down through generations, prepared with authentic techniques
  • Premium Ingredients: Carefully sourced, fresh ingredients from local markets and specialty suppliers
  • Intimate Setting: Private dining experiences in a warm, welcoming atmosphere
  • Cultural Storytelling: Learn about the history and significance of each dish while you dine

Join us for an experience that goes beyond dining—it's a celebration of culture, tradition, and the art of authentic Indian cooking.
